# Scorched Earth

Single-file HTML5 canvas sandbox. You are an unkillable flying supe with laser vision. The city — its people, its buildings, its helicopters — is your playground.

**Controls:** WASD / arrows fly (speed builds the longer you fly) · mouse aims · hold click for laser vision (drills through buildings) · double-tap a direction to dash-punch (hold a second direction for diagonals) · dash down into the street for a ground slam · Space for a thunderclap shockwave · M mute · P pause

Blood is permanent. Buildings collapse. The damage bill is itemized.

Open `index.html` in a browser, or play the deployed GitHub Pages build.
